# 🚗 Rent-Vehicle Community Marketplace

## Simple & Easy Project Scope

## 1️⃣ Project Idea (In Simple Words)

This is a website where:

* **Vehicle owners** can list their bikes/cars for rent
* **Renters** can search, book, and pay online
* The **platform manages bookings, payments, reviews, and safety**

👉 Goal: **Help people rent vehicles easily & help owners earn money**

---

## 2️⃣ Who Will Use This Platform? (User Roles)

### 👤 Guest (Not Logged In)

* View vehicles
* Search vehicles
* See prices & details
* Register or Login

---

### 🚘 Renter (Customer)

* Search vehicles
* Book vehicles
* Pay online
* View booking history

* Manage profile

---

### 🚙 Vehicle Owner (Host)

* Add vehicles for rent
* Set price & availability
* Accept or reject bookings
* Earn money
* Manage vehicles

### 🛠 Admin (Platform Owner)

* Manage users
* Approve vehicles

* Track revenue
* Control pricing & commission
